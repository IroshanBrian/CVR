Welcome To Ceylon Voyager's Retreat

Welcome to Ceylon Voyager's Retreat, a HOSTEL CONCEPT offering new and budget-friendly accommodation experience that emphasizes shared facilities. Guests enjoy dormitory-style rooms with multiple beds, sharing bathrooms, kitchens, and common areas. This communal setup fosters social interaction, creating a vibrant atmosphere where travelers can meet new people from around the world and share experiences. Our dedicated team of experienced professionals ensures a home away from home experience amidst the breathtaking beauty of nature, providing the best possible services.


At Ceylon Voyager's Retreat, we believe in the power of exceptional hospitality and the serenity of our natural surroundings. Our retreat is strategically located just one hour from the Colombo airport Katunayake, 10 minutes from the railway station and 15 minutes to the highway, ensuring easy access for our guests. Our experienced staff members are committed to creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere throughout your stay. From the moment you arrive, our attentive team will be there to cater to your needs and make your experience truly extraordinary.


Immerse yourself in the tranquility of our retreat, nestled amidst breathtaking landscapes. The lush greenery, gentle breezes, and harmonious sounds of nature will soothe your soul and invigorate your senses, providing a serene backdrop for your stay. We offer thoughtfully designed accommodations suitable for various types of travelers, whether you're seeking a romantic getaway, a family adventure, or a peaceful retreat. Our personalized services ensure that your stay is tailored to your preferences, making it a memorable experience.

Embark on an unforgettable journey with us at Ceylon Voyager's Retreat. Let our retreat be your haven as you explore the wonders of Sri Lanka. Contact us today and allow us to create a seamless connection between comfort, nature, and hospitality during your stay.
