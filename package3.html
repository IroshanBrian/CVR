Golden Fellowship

Booking Now

140$US / 2 Adults / 2 Nights / CMB Airport pick and drop. /Mirigama Railway Station pickup and drop

Duration: 2 Nights on BB basis (Arrival night and night before departure from Sri Lanka) add more days will cost 50$US per day.
Capacity: 2 persons
Bed: 1 Double Beds or two single beds as prefer, shared room (Private room on availability)
Services: Pickup and drop from, to airport by a car, one free trip to the city per day, Shared bathroom and toilet, Free WIFI, Free drop and pickup to and from the Railway Station,

Welcome to our accommodation, where your comfort is our top priority. Our rooms feature comfortable beds with quality mattresses and clean bed covers for a restful night's sleep. Bathrooms are regularly cleaned and stocked with towels, toilet paper, bathing soap, and a hairdryer. In addition to our room service, we provide lounge areas for relaxation and socializing, as well as a communal kitchen equipped with cookers. Stay connected with free Wi-Fi and take advantage of charging electric sockets for up to two devices. Each room also comes with a locker cupboard for secure storage.

Our 24/7 on-call help desk ensures prompt assistance whenever you need it. You can find all the mentioned services detailed on our website. Take a moment to unwind in our serene garden area, and rest assured that we offer complimentary transportation in case of emergencies. We are committed to providing a comfortable and convenient experience for all our guests, and we look forward to hosting you.

Special Note:

We have carefully designed this package to cater to your convenience, focusing on the first night upon your arrival and the second night before your departure. This all-in-one package includes 2 Nights on a BB basis, CMB Airport transfers, Mirigama Railway station transfers, and two city trips. By booking this package, you can enjoy unparalleled savings compared to booking these services separately. Seize this incredible opportunity and relish a seamless travel experience, with the assurance that everything is meticulously taken care of for your utmost comfort and convenience.
