About Us

Ceylon Voyager's Retreat

Welcome to Ceylon Voyager's Retreat, a HOSTEL CONCEPT offering new and budget-friendly accommodation experience that emphasizes shared facilities. Guests enjoy dormitory-style rooms with multiple beds, sharing bathrooms, kitchens, and common areas. This communal setup fosters social interaction, creating a vibrant atmosphere where travelers can meet new people from around the world and share experiences. Our dedicated team of experienced professionals ensures a home away from home experience amidst the breathtaking beauty of nature, providing the best possible services.

What We Do

Discover Our Services

Travel Plan

We are delighted to offer personalized travel plans tailored to your preferences. Whether it's a luxury getaway or a budget adventure, we can arrange it all within the country on request. We provide free information about the top highlights and recommendations, ensuring a memorable journey. Share your dream trip with us, and let's make it a reality.

Cuisines

Experience the taste of Sri Lanka exclusively with our culinary offerings. We take pride in providing a diverse range of Sri Lankan cuisines to suit every food preference, be it vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and more. As part of our package, enjoy a complimentary breakfast featuring an enticing selection of traditional Sri Lankan dishes to kickstart your day on a flavorful note.

Customizable Rest Experience

At our establishment, we prioritize comfort and privacy for our guests. In our sharing rooms, each individual is provided with their own separate bed, ensuring a restful stay. Additionally, we offer the flexibility to accommodate different needs, including the arrangement of family twin beds or king-sized beds upon request, further enhancing your sleeping experience.

Laundry

Our laundry service is designed to offer convenience and affordability. With our provided washing machine, clients can take care of their laundry needs at a minimal cost of just US$0.07 per item. This covers the usage of washing powder, liquid detergent, fabric conditioner, and the necessary energy consumption, ensuring a hassle-free laundry experience for our valued guests.

Hire Drive

Our packages go the extra mile with convenient pickups and drops. We understand the importance of seamless travel, which is why we offer complimentary up and down transfers from the railway station, along with a free up and down trip to the nearest city, per person every day throughout their stay. Sit back, relax, and enjoy the perks of hassle-free transportation with us.

Bar & Drink

We prioritize a responsible approach to beverages and strictly adhere to a policy of not selling alcohol. However, we do have our mini bar stocked with a variety of local beers and spirits, NOT FOR SALE but available for guests to enjoy and refill at their convenience. This ensures a refreshing and enjoyable experience without promoting alcohol consumption. We highly respect the relaxation and freedom expectations of all our guests, ensuring their experience remains undisturbed.
